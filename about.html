Who we are

Compassionate, ethical, & patient‑centered

Soroti Orthopaedic and Surgical Specialists Centre Limited (SOSSCL) was established with a vision to provide world-class orthopaedic and surgical care to eastern Uganda. We combine clinical excellence with a deep respect for every person who walks through our doors.

Our story: from vision to reality

Foundation

Founded by Dr. Michael Emusugut and a group of orthopaedic specialists, SOSSCL opened with a commitment to providing accessible, high-quality orthopaedic care to the region.

Building infrastructure

Early growth focused on establishing dedicated clinical spaces, modern operating theatres, and 24/7 emergency services to meet growing community needs.

Comprehensive services

Expanded to include a full-service laboratory, physiotherapy department, diagnostic imaging, and specialized outpatient clinics. Became a referral centre for orthopaedic excellence.

Centre of excellence

Today SOSSCL operates as a modern, integrated facility with a multi-disciplinary team serving the region with orthopaedic, surgical, and diagnostic services with compassion and excellence.

Read more about our journey

Community outreach: Introduced free orthopaedic camps and educational programs in surrounding districts to reach underserved communities.

Professional development: Established a nursing scholarship fund and ongoing training programs for healthcare workers in the region.

Continuous growth: Investing in modern infrastructure, including advanced diagnostic equipment and rehabilitation facilities to enhance patient outcomes.

Our mission

To deliver accessible, high‑quality, and compassionate orthopaedic and surgical care to every patient, with a focus on ethics, professionalism, and community well‑being.

Our vision

To be the leading orthopaedic and surgical centre in eastern Uganda – trusted for clinical excellence, innovation, and patient‑centred warmth.

Our values

Compassion, integrity, excellence, respect, and community. These guide every interaction and decision.

Beyond our walls: community first

We believe healthcare extends to the community. SOSSCL organises free medical camps, health education in schools, and supports vulnerable patients through our social fund.

  • Annual orthopaedic camp in Katakwi (500+ patients screened)
  • School scoliosis screening program
  • Emergency fund for those unable to pay
  • Training local village health teams
➕ details on past outreach

In 2025 alone, we held 4 camps, treated 1,200+ patients, and donated mobility aids to 80 individuals. Our team also volunteers at local orphanages.
