Soroti Orthopaedic And Surgical Specialists Center Limited (SOSSCL)

A patient-centered healthcare organization committed to delivering accessible, high-quality, and compassionate medical services. We combine modern medical practices with a strong focus on ethics, professionalism, and community well-being.

Read more about our philosophy

Our values: Integrity, compassion, excellence, and community. Since our founding, we have served thousands of patients from Soroti and beyond, ensuring that every person receives respectful, top-tier care regardless of background. We continuously invest in training and modern equipment.

Our vision: To be the leading orthopaedic and surgical centre in eastern Uganda, trusted for clinical excellence and patient warmth.

Our Services

Comprehensive medical services under one roof, delivered with professionalism and empathy.

Consultation & General Medicine

Comprehensive medical consultations for all ages, focusing on diagnosis, treatment, and prevention. Personalized care and ongoing support.

Orthopaedic & General Surgery

Expert care for bone, joint, and musculoskeletal conditions. From evaluation to recovery, we ensure safe, effective treatment.

Laboratory & Pharmacy

Reliable pharmacy and lab services for accurate diagnosis. Timely results and quality medications with proper guidance.
